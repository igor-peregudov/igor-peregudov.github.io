Brest Fortress in the cinema

Cinema satisfies the age-old need of the collective unconscious - people's need for common memories.

The film tells about the heroic defense of Brest Fortress, which took the first blow of German fascist invaders on June 22, 1941. With documentary accuracy the events are described that took place in the first days of defense.
